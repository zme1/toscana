seek new applicants and to continually promote
the growth of the Tuscan Brotherhood.

Thursday December 17, 1925 at 9 p.m. our
regular general meeting is brought to order, presided over
by our president Michele Simonetti. The roll call
returned the absences of the following officers: Second
Curator Adelmo Dati, Third Curator Lorenzo Lunardini,
Health Officer Giuseppe Fredianelli, and Room Guard
Eugenio Merciadri. Last meeting’s minutes were approved as
read. The two new applicants, Alfredo Tamburi and
Federigo Barsanti, were admitted. The president of the Ball [Committee]
Enemia De Santi announced that the date of the ball had been
fixed for the night of Tuesday February 16th at the Elk Club,
and that in order for it to be a success the committee needed the
cooperation of everybody. With that, members Michele Simonetti, Carlo Silvioni,
Jacopo Sodini, Romeo Maffei, Santino Pasquinelli, Alfredo
Vannucci, and Lorenzo Lunardini all volunteered themselves to
try and sell the advertising booklets. It was proposed by
Jacopo Sodini to send a letter to those members late on payments
with their respective amounts due, and that whoever does not
settle their debt at the next meeting should be dismissed. Proposed
by Santino Pasquinelli and seconded by Abramo Marsili to give
$10.00 to the youngster who operates the elevator. Proposed by Sam Marsili
and seconded by Olinto Cinelli to provide refreshments to the new administration
The following bills were paid: $30.00 to Rizieri Menchetti as a
subsidy, $4.00 to Adriano Puccetti as a subsidy, $11.25 for
room rent, $27.90 for refreshments at Pasquinelli’s, $35.53 to the
Secretary of Finance for one year, $6.93 to the Secretary of Correspondence for three months,
$7.10 to the Collector, $9.00 to the Treasurer. Deposited by the Secretary
of Finance: $45.50. In the cashbox today: $4553.55. The president declared
the vote for the three Curators open, to be chosen from the
6 nominees. [There were] 25 Voters. The elected officers were: First Curator Giuseppe
Fredianelli with 16 votes, Second Curator Americo Pasquinelli with
12 votes, and Third Curator Olinto Cinelli with 11 votes. Eugenio Merciadri
defeated his opponent Ezio Carli for Room Guard.
Having nothing else to attend to, the president declared the
meeting adjourned. Time 11:30 p.m.
