Seduta Ordinaria del 20 Gennaio 1921

General Meeting of January 20, 1921.
At 8:30 p.m. President Stefano Maffei
called the meeting to order, and after the reading
of the minutes the President put it
to the approval of the assembly and it was
approved it as it was written.
We passed to the general expense report after
all bills had been paid. There remains
a net balance of $1525.48 in the Treasurer’s cashbox.
This being the last meeting that President
Stefano Maffei [would] preside over for the year 1920,
he inducted the new administration,
and called on the new president
Sam Marsili as well as all of the new
administration, and with kind words wished them good
fortune during their tenure
for the year 1921.

Thursday January 19, 1922 at 9.00 p.m.
President Sam Marsili brought the
meeting to order, and after the previous meeting’s
minutes were read they were put to a vote and
are not approved as read because there was cause
for renewed discussion regarding
Brother Michele Simonetti’s operation,
so the issue is left to the next meeting,
and a committee was chosen to investigate
if, according to our regulations, it was just to
pay him $50.00 for expenses incurred fro
the operation. Stefano Maffei, Casimirro Pellegrini
Eugenio Bertoni, and Castruccio Cappellini [make up] the committee.
A medical subsidy of $8.00 was granted
to the ex-president Sam Marsili.
The application for admission and participation
in this League was presented for Angelo
Barducci, 34, and it was accepted.
Then we passed to the dismissal of members
who found themselves to be more than (12) months
