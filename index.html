Welcome to the project site for the Lega Toscana di Protezione. This research was conducted by four student researchers and one faculty researcher from the Department of French and Italian and with consultation from the Advanced DH Praxis group, led by professor Elisa Beshero-Bondar at the University of Pittsburgh, Greensburg campus.

This research project is a multi-dimensional look into the members who comprised the Lega Toscana di Protezione, a Pittsburgh-based beneficial group that was exclusivel to Italian immigrants living in the Pittsburgh area. Using archived documents from the Heinz History Center as primary sources, the researchers combed through minute logs, application materials, and newspaper entries to construct a composite portrait of what this group was, and who ascribed to it.

Read more about our researchers, our questions, and how we approached them in our "About us" section above.
